The Goal of the Guide:

This guide will walk you through installing Tale of Two Wastelands, a total conversion mod that merges Fallout New Vegas and Fallout 3 together, as well as installing essential mods for a perfectly stable and smooth experience. There will also be some optional gameplay/content mods at the end of the guide.

Requirements:

  • An English copy of FNV and FO3 with all the DLC from either Steam or GOG (Instructions for properly installing them will be in the next step)
    • Only the English versions of FNV/FO3 are supported. If your game is not English, follow these steps to switch it
    • The Bethesda.net version has a recompiled executable and therefore will not work with this guide
  • Windows 7 or higher (64bit)
  • A text editor (I recommend Notepad++, but default Windows notepad will work)
  • An archiving tool (I recommend 7-Zip)
  • Latest GPU drivers (Either NVIDIA or AMD)
  • At least 22 gigabytes of free drive space
  • A Nexus Mods account
